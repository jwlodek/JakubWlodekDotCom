Focus Stacked 3D Reconstruction

During an internship at Brookhaven National Laboratory, I wrote software that used focus stacking as a way of collecting high quality images with a shallow depth of field fixed microscope, as well as recreating three dimensional depth data. The image to the left shows some of my results. This project is discussed further in my publications which can be found in the Resume/CV portion of this website.
