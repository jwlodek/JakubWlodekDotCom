About Me!

Welcome to my website! My name is Jakub Wlodek, and I am a Computer Science and Applied Mathematics double major at Stony Brook University, currently in my senior year. I work at Brookhaven National Laboratory as a student assistant for the controls group at the National Synchrotron Light Source II. My work primarily revolves around writing Computer Vision based tools for scientists, along with developing new plugins and detector drivers for the open source area detector project. If you would like to learn more about these projects, make sure to visit the "Projects" page, and visit github for source code. Outside of work and school, I have a passion for all things technological, and I also greatly enjoy spending time outside or on the water. In fact, I even row on the Stony Brook University Crew team.

I also enjoy travelling, and have visited many countries in Europe, including Poland (where my family is from), Hungary, France, Germany, and Slovakia.
